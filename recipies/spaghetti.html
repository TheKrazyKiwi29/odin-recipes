Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Butter
  • Cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water, then drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the baking dish, then spread half of the noodles in the bottom.
  3. Top with the mixture of cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream.
  4. Cover with remaining spaghetti and butter.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, top with Cheese, and continue baking until the cheese is melted.
